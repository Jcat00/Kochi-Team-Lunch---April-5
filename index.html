★ Our Recommendation — Option 1
Dhe Puttu
NH Bypass, Edappally · 5 min from office · Opens 11:30 AM · 📞 +91 89433 42494 · dheputtu.com

Dhe Puttu is Kochi's most iconic puttu restaurant and the kind of place every local knows by name — which instantly gives the team a shared reference point. The menu elevates Kerala's beloved puttu into creative combos, all named after Malayalam films. The moment the team sits down, they're welcomed with complimentary Sulaimani tea and unlimited pappadoms. It sets a warm, generous tone before anyone has even ordered. More importantly, taking a new team here sends a clear message: we are rooted in this city, and we're here to have fun. That's exactly the right note for a first lunch together.

Option 2
Roastown Global Grill
Bypass Junction, Ponekkara · 5 min from office · Opens 12:00 PM · 📞 +91 77360 58333 · roastownglobal.com

Roastown is the right choice if you want the lunch to feel more like an event. Currently one of Kochi's most talked-about restaurants, it's a 165-seater with three live kitchens, a pizzeria, and a chef's studio. The menu takes the table on a global tour — Argentina, Morocco, Korea, Vietnam, France. For a team still getting to know the city, the menu alone becomes a conversation. The interiors are polished and comfortable without being stiff. It delivers the energy of a celebration, without the formality of fine dining.

Option 3 — Extra
Kayees Biryani
Durbar Hall Rd, Pallimukku · ~20 min from office · Opens 11:00 AM · 📞 +91 91506 54321

Kayees is not just a restaurant. For the people of Kochi, it is an emotion. Founded in 1948, it has cult status in the city and has been serving the same iconic biryani for over 75 years. Every Kochi local has a Kayees story. Taking the team here is less about the meal and more about the initiation — it tells them: you are part of this city now. The practical note is distance: at 20 minutes from the office, the 12–1:30 PM window will need to be managed. We recommend this as a strong option if the team is up for a short drive that is very much worth it.

✦ The Kayees Biryani — What Makes It Unique
Kayikkante Biriyani

The biryani is cooked in a sealed pot — jeerakasala rice, slow-cooked with tender meat, ghee, raisins, cashews, and a notable addition: pineapple. It's sweetish, aromatic, and unlike anything else in Kochi. Served with the house dates chutney, it's a full sensory experience. Note: primarily a non-veg restaurant. Vegetarians can request plain ghee rice.
